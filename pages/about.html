I'm Rebecca, Crafting Digital Dreams

Since 2024, I've immersed myself in the world of web design and development, transforming complex problems into simple, beautiful, and intuitive solutions. My journey into coding began after feeling stuck in a data entry job that offered little in the way of fulfillment or growth. It was a conversation with my sister that sparked the idea to pursue web design—a field that allows me to merge my love for creativity and technology while working from home.

This shift not only aligned with my passion for designing things but also supported my commitment to homeschooling my son, Atticus, and spending time with my two beloved cats, Boots and Pumpkin. Outside the digital world, I find joy in the simple pleasures of life—crocheting intricate projects, bonding with animals (a lifelong love that began with studying veterinary science for exotic animals), and dreaming about our future home nestled in nature.

My love for animals runs deep, and I feel a profound connection with them, which is something that fuels my creativity and passion in everything I do. As I continue to grow in this field, I'm excited to share my journey through a blog where I’ll document my experiences in coding, the challenges and triumphs of building my own website, and snippets of my daily life.

My website is a canvas, where each pixel is a reflection of my skills, creativity, and the things that matter most to me. I’m eager to showcase various projects, offering a glimpse into my world of design and development.

Step into my world, where creativity meets code, and every pixel tells a story. Join me on this journey as I bring together technology, design, and the life I love—one pixel at a time.
